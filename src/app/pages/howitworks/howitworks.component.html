How it Works

Our Process is Simple

01
We ask about you
We require general information about you. We will inquire about where you work? Means of identification and other related details. We will want to know what you are planning towards, whether to buy a house, a car or saving towards your retirement. We will ask you what your minimum contribution will be and for how long are you willing to invest.
02
Choose your investment
Once you have met the required investment criteria, you can choose an investment portfolio of your choice and proceed to activate investment plan. You can also create an investment mix to maximize your potential earnings.
03
Our Technology will manage your funds
We will evaluate your contribution and give you real time insights into your potential earnings on selected investment plan. You will be able to monitor progress on a daily basis and also watch your funds grow.
